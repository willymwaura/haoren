    About Us

    At HaoRen, we started with a simple belief: everyone deserves skincare that's safe, effective, and kind to their skin. Founded to address the unique needs of all skin types, we set out to craft products that nurture and protect, fostering confidence with every use.

    Haoren Global: A Worldwide Fragrance Powerhouse

    Haoren's global operations reflect its commitment to excellence, innovation, and accessibility across continents. With strategically located offices and factories, Haoren ensures seamless production, distribution, and customer support worldwide.

    Haoren's global footprint ensures agility in sourcing, sustainability, and market responsiveness. From artisanal Vietnamese perfumes to German-engineered luxury, each location amplifies Haoren's mission: to craft scents that transcend borders.

    "Rooted in tradition, blooming worldwide."

    Kind to Skin, Crafted for Your World

    At Haoren, we believe luxury should never come at a cost to you, your skin, or our planet. Our fragrances are:
    1.Dermatologist-approved & formulated for all skin types, including sensitive skin
    2.Cruelty-free – we never test on animals, only on willing humans
    3.Tropical-tested to stay vibrant in African heat and humidity
    4.Free from parabens, harsh alcohols, and common irritants
